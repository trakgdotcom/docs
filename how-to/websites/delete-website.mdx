# Delete Website

You can delete any website you've added from your Trakg dashboard.

## How to Delete a Website

1. Go to your **[Dashboard → Websites](https://app.trakg.com/websites)**.
2. Find the website you want to remove.
3. Click on the **Edit** button.
4. Scroll to the bottom and click on the **Delete Website** option.

> ⚠️ **Warning:**  
> Deleting a website is permanent. All associated data including:
>
> - Tracked leads
> - Captured form data
> - Automations
> - Analytics history  
>
> will be **permanently deleted** and cannot be recovered.

Make sure you have exported or saved any critical data before deletion.
